CULTURAL AWARENESS

  As weather is to a place in the same analogy culture is to people even though in a more well defined manner and irrespective of time. Culture sets and defines the boundaries of various communities and helps in better understanding of the development of an individual. It in no way retrains the activity of an individual but brings about the unique out of the varied aspects of ones doing.  




Many mistake it to be a tribal restricted thing, but in a country like ours with every passing kilometer in a journey, you might witness the change in cultural behaviors of people from clothes to language to cuisine.

Lets take in a case for better understanding,
A North Indian finds a job in the southern part of India, what aspects he looks forward to before joining and to adjust in that particular area?
1. Firstly he’ll study about the location
2. Try learn the language to communicate with the locals
3. Study about the food served there and locations where he/ she could find their own staple food.
By the time the person shifts there, he/she knows about the beginners facts and still he/she takes time adjusting with various aspects of that place. All this complies, the Cultural adjustment of an individual.

Now the question is what exactly is Cultural awareness?
When defined cultural awareness, it’s the understanding of cultural differences and being aware about different cultural values, beliefs and perceptions.
This isn’t restricted to people who are keen to know things but also helps them in their workspace, where they communicate to build an even environment for people with different cultures.
It helps to reduce the intercultural competence, as it helps people to better understand what might lead to hurting somebody’s developmental sentiments before they put forward any statement. Hence reduces conflicts on whole.

Now question comes up, how to promote cultural awareness amongst people?
On grassroot level:
Mostly the best grassroot education of people begin when they are too young to corporate any difference, that is the school going time. If at that point they are trained to generate hate for cultures, they grow into an intolerant human being towards the varied cultures, hence following practices should be introduced in schools:
1. Promote students of different cultures, talk openly about it infront of the group.
2. Celebrating different cultural festivals and involve every student in it irrespective of the culture.
3. Letting students wear their cultural attires in the form of fancy dress.

Awareness:
To every aspect, the knowledge and tolerance toward an idea is very necessary. Hence spreading cultural awareness in the form of art is the best way, hence involving more and more people in cultural performances like native dances, promoting the use of their own language wherever they could and singing their native songs helps in better understanding.

Even the newly proposed policy of education i.e. NEP proposes the use of the local dialect to educate the masses so that they remember their cultural heritage in upcoming time too. Hence, everything holds the same importance as your own ideology learn to respect that.
