WOMEN EMPOWERMENT

  Through Women empowerment programs we want to anchor the autonomy of women by providing them opportunity of sustainable development. Our interventions are designed on understanding of importance of women in value chain. We have conducted vocational training like Sewing training, beautician courses, financial literacy program, Digital literacy to help women to achieve self-sufficiency and financial independence.

At RES we recognize that empowering women is one of the primary ways through which we can achieve our organizational goals and vision. It is thus that we’ve engaged ourselves in the past and continue to work on number of projects directly or indirectly targeting empowerment of women. It is through these programs that we aim to anchor the autonomy of women by providing them with sustainable opportunities ensuring their development. Our interventions are designed after careful analysis, study, need assessment surveys and so on, to increase the authenticity and impact of our projects. We have conducted vocational training like Sewing training, beautician courses, financial literacy programs, Digital literacy initiatives to help women achieve self-sufficiency and financial independence.

We believe that empowerment and autonomy of women along with improvement in their political, social, economic and health status is highly significant for achieving sustainable development in any part of the world. Today, Women are facing threats to their lives, health and well- being as a result of being overburdened with work and of their lack of power and influence in various aspects of life. Especially in countries like India, women receive less formal education than men, and at the same time, women's own knowledge, abilities and coping mechanisms often go unrecognized by the society. The power relations that impede women's attainment of healthy and fulfilling lives operate at many levels of society and thus women empowerment is critical factor in a developing country like India and it is through these following interventions that the organization is working towards women empowerment.

• Education is one of the most important means of empowering women with knowledge, skills and self-confidence necessary to participate fully in the development process, so it is a focus area in our initiatives.

• Our programs also target rural men and women’s conscious raising and awareness building initiatives to educate their girl child and non-discriminatory attitude in providing quality life and future for both girls and boys.

• Skill development initiatives are a major aspect of our interventions for women which helps to create better employment opportunities and increase the income generation per household.

• Promoting women’s health through regular camps, workshops on child-bearing, follow-ups and so on.

Renaissance Education Society(R.E.S.) is a non-profit organisation registered under the society act XXI, 1860, was set up in Delhi in 2005. We are comitted towards shaping a positive change and working as catalust for bringing social and exonomic change for underprivileged communities.
