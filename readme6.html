   True education is beyond earning degrees it is more than bookish knowledge. Education means inculcating moral values, positive thinking, attitude of helping, attitude of giving to society and ethical values these kind of students are only able to bring changes in society.

Education gives us a knowledge of the world around us and changes it into something better. It develops in us a perspective of looking at life. It helps us build opinions and have points of view on things in life. People debate over the subject of whether education is the only thing that gives knowledge.

Higher levels of education are associated with a wide range of positive outcomes - including better health and wellbeing, higher social trust, greater political interest, lower political cynicism, and less hostile attitudes towards immigrants.

Through Education programs we are aiming to equip children with knowledge, skills and interpersonal competences required for their development. Our interventions are focused on different aspects of education sector for holistic development of children.

RES education program is focus on improving the access to quality education by educational infrastructure development, digital literacy, develop smart class and libraries.

Infrastructure is a core component of any educational institution, especially for schools as they are a second home for children and play a very crucial role in their development. Good Infrastructure aids in developing a healthy comfort level among the students which in turn contributes to the development of safe spaces for children. It is believed that good infrastructure inspires young learners to dream big and enjoy their learning environment leaving a long-lasting positive impact on their holistic development.

Ro Purifier - A healthy mind resides in a healthy body and thus a clean and healthy lead-free water is a necessity in protecting the health of young learners giving their bodies the proper nutrients and minerals. A good quality RO prevents bacterial infections and other contaminated water related disease that often hamper their health.

Library - A well-equipped library with a mood-lifting ambience plays a very important role in encouraging students and developing their interest towards exploring different contours of knowledge.

Computer literacy- In the current globalized world the IT sector plays a huge role and the art of using computers efficiently and effectively is something that all students should know, it is a need of the hour. The Computer Literacy classes shall help the students to learn basic computer skills and applications which will help them in their everyday lives and further aid them in their learning and growth.
