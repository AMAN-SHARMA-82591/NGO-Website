ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

Renaissance Education Society (R.E.S.) is a non-profit organisation registered under the society act XXI, 1860, was set up in Delhi in 2005. We are committed towards shaping a positive change and working as catalyst for bringing social and economic change for underprivileged communities. We are currently operating successfully in National Capital Region.

WHAT WE DO

There are various sectors that our NGO Renaissance Education Society works for. From skill development, women empowerment to spreading awareness about health and safety issues along with water sanitation and environment sustainability have been our field of work for more than a decade. Our interventions are sustainable and scalable based on holistic approach.

CORE VALUE

Our values are important because they help us to grow and develope.The decisions we make are a reflection of our values and beliefs, and they are always directed towards a specific purpose. That purpose is the satisfaction of our individual or collective (organizational needs).

INTEGRITY

Integrity is the core quality of a successful and happy life. Having integriy means being totally honest and truthful in every part of your life. And it is integrity that enhances all your other values. The quality of person you are is determined by how well you live up to the values that are most important to you.

INNOVATION

Innovation in its modern meaning is "a new idea, creative thoughts, and new imaginations in form of device or method". Innovation is often also viewed as the application of better solutions that meet new requirements, unarticulated needs, or existing markets needs. The opposite of innovation is exnovation.

COMMITMENT

Commitment to work or work commitment is defined as the level of enthusiasm as employee has towards his/her tasks assigned at a workplace. It is the feeling of responsibility that a person has towards the goals, mission, and vision of the organization he/she is associated with.
