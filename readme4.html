PUBLIC HEALTH

   Millions of people die in India due to insufficient access to health services, poor quality care and non- utilisation of available health schemes because of lack of awareness. Our focus is to improve access of healthcare services along with quality care. We promote access to affordable and sustainable health services to beneficiaries by providing health counselling, organising health camps, operating Mobile health van, conducting awareness program on various health issues and sensitizing the community about various government running health schemes.

We at RES believe that healthcare should be accessible, affordable and available to all, for it is a basic human right and no section of the population should be depraved of quality healthcare; be it infrastructural, community, primary or tertiary healthcare platforms. It is our core belief that healthcare is a necessity and an individualâ€™s socio-economic status should not impact the availability of healthcare to certain sections of the population.

With this attitude the organization has adopted a manifold approach to tackle various aspects of healthcare in different parts of the country impacting diverse sections of the population. It includes community level initiatives, capacity building initiatives, infrastructural initiatives, doorstep healthcare services, Mobile Medical Units, awareness raising, free medical camps across the country and so on; we even work on different forms of non-mainstream illnesses which includes counselling services and other forms of palliative care as deliverables for the economically weaker sections.

Through our various initiatives the impact has varied as well including increased nutrition levels for pregnant women and children, building sensitization in the community through building a network of strong volunteer base and awareness raising initiatives in the field of health, hygiene and sanitation. Our initiative of Mobile Medical Units and Doorstep healthcare delivery services we have targeted among others the old-aged population and providing them with easy and accessible healthcare system delivery reaching out to them personally and even take constant follow-ups with the impacted population to ensure optimum positive results.

Renaissance Education Society(R.E.S.) is a non-profit organisation registered under the society act XXI, 1860, was set up in Delhi in 2005. We are comitted towards shaping a positive change and working as catalust for bringing social and exonomic change for underprivileged communities.
