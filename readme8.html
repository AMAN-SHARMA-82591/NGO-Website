ENVIORMENTAL AWARENESS

   Ever wondered why people on environment campaigns speak out loud, “SAVE ENVIRONMENT, SAVE LIVES”?
Ever thought if all the natural resources come to an end, will we be able to survive?
Ever thought if the oxygen in air depletes, how much will it cost for an essential thing like it to for every second of our lives?
There are so many similar questions but so little answers to it.  




People have got in terms with so much of comfort and low cost measures, that the they’ve forgotten there’s something far more advanced and essential thing is getting affected. The answer to this is obvious but people put heed to more materialistic things than the ones that led them to be what they are right now, i.e. the Environment.

There’s a saying, Man has become his own enemy. This idiom has various aspects for us to see but the most important of us all which doesn’t really vary with places, time, gender or ethnicities is Harnessing the environment that we’ve been given in bonus to the life we’re leading.

Now the question is what really is Environmental awareness?
Technically if we get into definitions, it means to understand the fragility of our environment and the importance of its protection. To elaborate it means that the people spread in the name:
AWARENESS
METHODS OF PROTECTION
DIVERSE EFFECTS CAUSED SO FAR AND EXPECTED IN FUTURE
For this purpose, the United Nations even proposed a day on 5 th June every year as WORLD ENVIRONMENTS DAY in which many people from individual level to schools to organizations bring about various campaigns for awareness of the people to planting trees.

How to come forward and bring about awareness on grassroot level?
Awareness:
1. Many people need to be reminded of what it might look like in upcoming years if the same irresponsibility continues, there’s no going back
2. Make them aware about the natural resources and their daily activities that have led to its deficit.
3. How extinction has led to imbalance of ecology.
4. Various related hazards related to it:
Global warming
Ozone layer depletion
Many other effects that have started doing the damage

Introduction of the three R’s:
1. Reduce
2. Reuse
3. Recycle

Awareness at grassroot levels:
In schools, building the basic habits and inculcating good and environment savior habits can be promoted by:
1. Asking them to switch off the electrical appliances when not in use.
2. Promoting clean drives in the school campus itself.
3. Telling them the use of Three coloured dustbin.
4. Banning the use of plastic in school premises.

Hence, this Earth is ours and taking it for granted may now seem a better choice for now but in coming years, this lavish and ungrateful style of living is going to pay back but negatively. Better take precautions before rather than regretting later of what could have been done for the savior.
